Who We Are

Untapped Creative is a Portland, OR based design studio made up of makers, strategists, day dreamers and risk takers. Fueled by empathy and passion for what we do, we’re unique in our structure in that while you always get a team to support you, our founder is the primary content creator for every client, keeping us consistent, personally committed, and free to make our best work.
