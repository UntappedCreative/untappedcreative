Building Brands with Purpose

We're a design studio based in Portland, OR, specializing in brand design and marketing communications. We focus on data-driven and human-centered design practices, insuring every decision we make has a clear reason and purpose. We thrive in either print or digital spaces, whatever's the most effective way to engage with your unique audience. Our loyalty runs deep, and by always putting our customers (and ultimately their customers) first, we deliver designs and experiences to be proud of.

Hi, I'm Danny Wethern

founder & creative lead at Untapped Creative

I've been a graphic and web designer for over 6 years, and I've had the pleasure to work with more than 80 clients during my career, both locally and internationally. Here at Untapped, I'm the creative lead on every project, and our other designers are brought on whenever their skill set helps drive a project to success. This keeps our costs lower, our relationship with clients more personal, and our capabilities more wide spread.
