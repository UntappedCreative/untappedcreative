Fueled by Empathy.

While passion and empathy fuel our creative drive, our studio structure is what sets us apart. We keep our team's small and our intention's focused on building meaningful connections with your unique audience. This not only increases our productivity, but the impact our work has for our client's. It all gets a lot easier when it's a 'we' thing, and not a 'for them' thing.

More About our Process

How We Work:

Tailored Solutions

There isn't a template for what we do (even though sometimes we'd appreciate that). Every business, brand and product is different and we strive to share that story in a way people can connect with.

Small (but powerful) Teams

Our founder, Danny Wethern, is the primary content creator for every client. From there, individual team members are brought on whenever their expertise helps drive the project to success. Keeping our team small and consistent for every project means we're always committed and uniquely engaged with our client's business.

Pushing Expectations

We don't just produce work for clients, we partner with them. We're here to help in any way we can, while being respectful of peoples time and budgets. We're a young team ready to leave our stamp on the industry; whenever we have a chance to over-deliver, we take it.

Hi! I'm Danny Wethern

Founder & Creative Director

I'm a creative director, designer and routine over-thinker with over 5 years of experience building brand identities and marketing initiatives. I've worked with over 60 clients, from large corporations needing comprehensive rebrands, to small start-ups and family businesses. While my style usually is to keep things modern and simplified, I love the process of diving into client's unique atmosphere and making design decisions that relate to their audience. For those curious, I received a BFA in graphic and web design from the Art Institute of Portland (R.I.P.). My work has been featured in Ascender's Volume 1: Masters of Contemporary Lettering, Coroflot's homepage, Udemy, and I'm approved as a Fiverr Pro.
