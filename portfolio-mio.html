Made in Oregon

Web Design & Campaign Development

The Pioneer Way.

Made In Oregon was the first business focusing on inventory that strictly came from their state. Starting in the 1970's, they've steadily grown to multiple stores throughout the state, predominently in Portland, OR. Their online sales also continue to grow year over year. With over 1000 active SKUs, they carry gifts that touch every style and personality.

While Made In Oregon has continued to grow, the company hasn't invested heavily in their branding. Their website was slightly outdated, and we were given the oppotunity to change that. Our efforts were focused primarily on the website, as well as supporting MIO on their campaign executions with email design and other digital assets.

Photography was provided from both our cities and our beautiful landscapes, encompassing what makes Oregon unique. Illustrations were used to cohesively blend very different assets, and instantly made visuals for the site more approachable. We developed several programs with MIO, including the Signature Vendor and Coporate Sales programs. These differentiated these promotions from typical collateral, and further pushed the brand to create a deeper user experience.

Made In Oregon saw an instant jump in click and open rate on emails we developed, and the brand's various emails and communications all started becoming cohesive. We're all excited to see how the Corporate Sales program performs, and we continue to connect with the rich and unique culture of the pacific NW.
