Neo Assist

Website Redesign & Brand Direction

Ridiculous Tech Made Approachable.

Neo Assist is a customer support company based out of Sau Paulo, Brazil. They supply companies with a self automated chat support bot, as well as a series of other tools for customer relations. Neo Assist wanted their website to more accurately reflect the tech side of their business, while also delivering a better user experience, especially for American users.

Neo Assist began offering more complex and intelligent services for their customer base, and needed a website that reflected their sophisticated use of technology. Neo Assist also began hosting annual customer support expoâ€™s in Brazil (called Nexus) and needed appropriately designed invitations to showcase how important this years event would be. The client had a newly established logo, but no examples or guidance existed for how to show the new identity.

A system of shapes were developed, based heavily off the cloud logo that the client had just had designed by another agency. The friendly and approachable nature of the logo led to a more established look for the brand. Custom made icons and subtle complexities in the use of patterns all kept these principles in mind, leading the way for the future of a cohesive brand experience for the clients customers.

Neo Assist had a successful launch and tradeshow application, and continue to push the standards of customer support. Their new brand has grown and their identity has shifted beautifully with it, now expressing their fun and approachable nature more clearly than ever.
